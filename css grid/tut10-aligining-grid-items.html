Aligining Grid Items

Properties used:-

  1. Vertical Alignment
    1. align-items
    2. align-self

    Properties

    • start(top)
    • end(bottom)
    • center
    • stretch(normal/defalut value)
  2. Horizontal Alignment
    1. justify-items
    2. justify-self

    Properties

    • start(left)
    • end(right)
    • center
    • stretch(normal/defalut value)
  3. place-items (short hand of align-items and justify-items)
  4. place-self (short hand of align-self and justify-self)

SYNTAX===== place-items:align-items, justify-items;

EXAMPLE==== place-items:center, start;
